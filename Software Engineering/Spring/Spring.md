An ecosystem of frameworks that include: [[Spring Core]], [[Spring MVC]], [[Spring Data Access]], [[Spring Testing]]. [[Spring Core]] acts as the centerpiece of the ecosystem, like the "sun" is for a solar system. All of the frameworks are packaged nicely by [[Spring Boot]] to remove barrier of entry.
